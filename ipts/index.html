FAQ

Is there any entry fee to be a part of this contest?

No, the event is free of cost.

I don't know much about coding can I participate?

Yes you can, we organize these events just for you so that you can start coding and improve your skills.

How many rounds are there in the event?

There will be two rounds in the event.There will be increase of difficulty level proceeding from round 1 to round 2.

Why should we participate in this event?

Participating in such events improves your coding skills and brings you the opportunity to kickstart your journey in competitive programming.

Is any pre-requisite knowledge needed to take part in this event?

Yes, you should know basics of atleast one programming language.
